Pickling reference

All batches — prep, brine, aromatics, storage & shelf life

Phase 1 — Cold prep
Small cucumbers & slicing cucumbers
PrepWash. Slice slicers into rounds; leave small cukes whole or cut into spears. Trim blossom ends completely. Ice bath.
Cold prep
Jalapeños & mixed peppers
PrepWear gloves. Slice into uniform 1/4-inch rings or strips. Set aside in bowl.
Cold prep
Sweet onions
PrepPeel and thinly slice into half-moons. Set aside.
Cold prep
Fresh okra
PrepWash thoroughly. Keep whole with stems attached to prevent sliminess.
Cold prep
Green tomatoes
PrepWash. Slice into 1/4-inch rounds or wedges. Firm, unripe tomatoes only.
Cold prep
Cucumbers — Szechuan
PrepWash. Smash whole with the flat of a knife, then tear into rough chunks. Salt and rest 15 min, pat dry.
Cold prep
Daikon, carrots & cucumbers — Bánh Mì
PrepPeel daikon and carrots. Julienne or cut into thin matchsticks. Slice cucumbers into thin rounds. Salt all, rest 10 min, rinse and pat dry.
Cold prep
Phase 2 — Hot prep
Garden beets
PrepLeave skins and roots on. Boil until tender. Cool in ice water, slip skins off with fingers, slice into wedges.
Hot prep
Roasted jalapeños (oven / broil)
PrepLeave whole. Place on a rack over a foil-lined sheet pan. Broil on high, 4-6 inches from element, turning once, until fully blistered and charred on both sides. About 8-12 minutes total. Do not peel. Cool slightly, then slice into rings or leave whole depending on use.
Use forRoasted cowboy candy, or as an optional add-in (2-3 rings per pint jar) to any spicy cucumber recipe.
Oven / broil
Phase 3 — Assembly & brine
Small cucumbers & okra
PackPack tightly and vertically into clean pint jars.
Ratio1:1 — 3 cups acid : 3 cups water
BrineApple cider vinegar + 2 tbsp sugar + 3 tbsp salt
Aromatics1 smashed garlic clove, 1 tsp dill seed, 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
Water bath can (10 min) or fridge1 year sealed / 2 months open
Garden beets & sweet onions
PackBeets in dedicated jars; sliced onions in separate jars.
Ratio2:1 — 3 cups acid : 1.5 cups water
BrineApple cider vinegar + 1.5 cups sugar + 1 tbsp salt
Aromatics1/2 tsp mustard seed, 1 whole clove, small pinch of allspice
Water bath can (10 min) or fridge1 year sealed / 2 months open
Slicing cucumbers — honey mustard
PackPull from ice bath, dry quickly, pack tightly into pint jars.
Ratio3:1 — 1.5 cups acid : 0.5 cups water
BrineWhite vinegar + 1/2 cup honey + 2 tbsp Dijon + 1 tbsp salt + 1/2 tsp turmeric
Aromatics1/2 tsp yellow mustard seeds, 1 smashed garlic clove
Refrigerator style (best for crunch)Fridge: 2 months
Jalapeños — cowboy candy (raw)
PackBoil slices directly in brine for exactly 4 minutes. Pack hot with slotted spoon.
RatioPure acid — 1.5 cups : 0 water
BrineApple cider vinegar + 1.5 cups sugar + 1/2 tsp garlic powder
Aromatics1/4 tsp celery seed
Refrigerator styleFridge: 3 months (high sugar preserves)
Jalapeños — cowboy candy (roasted)
PackBroil jalapeños whole until fully blistered. Slice into rings skin-on. Add to jars. Pour hot brine over immediately.
RatioPure acid — 1.5 cups : 0 water
BrineApple cider vinegar + 1.5 cups sugar + 1/2 tsp garlic powder + 1 tsp smoked paprika
Aromatics1/4 tsp celery seed, 1/4 tsp cumin
NoteSofter texture than raw-pack. Smoked paprika bridges the gap on smoke flavor.
Refrigerator styleFridge: 3 months
Szechuan cucumbers
PackPack smashed cucumber chunks loosely into jars. Sesame oil is a finishing drizzle only, not in the brine.
Ratio1:1 — 1 cup acid : 1 cup water
BrineRice vinegar + 1 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tbsp sugar + 1 tsp salt + 1 tsp chili oil
Aromatics1 tsp Szechuan peppercorns, 2 dried red chilies, 2 smashed garlic cloves
FinishDrizzle 1/2 tsp toasted sesame oil per jar before serving.
Optional: add 2-3 roasted jalapeño rings per jar for deeper heat.
Refrigerator styleFridge: 3 weeks (best at 48 hrs)
Habanero garlic cucumbers
PackPack cucumber spears vertically. Add whole habaneros between spears.
Ratio1:1 — 2 cups acid : 2 cups water
BrineWhite vinegar + 1 tbsp sugar + 2 tbsp salt
Aromatics4 smashed garlic cloves, 2 whole habaneros per jar, 1/2 tsp black peppercorns
Optional: add 2-3 roasted jalapeño rings per jar for layered heat.
Water bath can (10 min) or fridge1 year sealed / 2 months open
Dill & coriander seed cucumbers
PackPack spears or rounds tightly and vertically into pint jars.
Ratio1:1 — 3 cups acid : 3 cups water
BrineApple cider vinegar + 1 tbsp sugar + 3 tbsp salt
Aromatics1 tsp dill seed, 1 tsp whole coriander seed, 1 smashed garlic clove, 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
Optional: add 2-3 roasted jalapeño rings per jar for charred heat against the bright dill.
Water bath can (10 min) or fridge1 year sealed / 2 months open
Green tomatoes
PackPack rounds or wedges firmly into pint jars.
Ratio1:1 — 2 cups acid : 2 cups water
BrineApple cider vinegar + 1 tbsp sugar + 2 tbsp salt
Aromatics1 smashed garlic clove, 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes, 1/2 tsp mustard seed, fresh dill sprig
Optional: add 2-3 roasted jalapeño rings per jar for a Southern-style spicy kick.
Water bath can (10 min) or fridge1 year sealed / 2 months open
Bánh Mì — daikon, carrot & cucumber
PackPack matchsticks and rounds loosely. Tuck lemongrass and cilantro stems along the sides of each jar.
RatioPure acid — 2 cups : 0 water
BrineRice vinegar + 4 tbsp white sugar + 1 tsp salt
Aromatics1 smashed lemongrass stalk, 4-5 fresh cilantro stems, 3-4 thin jalapeño or bird's eye chili slices
Refrigerator styleFridge: 3 weeks (best at 24 hrs)
